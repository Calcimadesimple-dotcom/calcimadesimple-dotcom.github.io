Understanding the Intrinsic Value (DCF) Calculator

Value investing relies on finding stocks trading below their true worth. Our Intrinsic Value Calculator uses the Reverse Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) method to estimate a company's fair value based on its Earnings Per Share (EPS) and projected growth rate.

By inputting your required rate of return (discount rate), this tool projects future corporate cash flows and discounts them back to today's value. If the calculated intrinsic value is significantly higher than the current stock price on the NSE or BSE, it indicates a potentially undervalued opportunity. This is an essential tool for long-term equity analysts and portfolio builders looking to maximize their Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR).
